# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members of the EquiPilot AI community pledge to make participation in our project a positive and respectful experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, race, religion or sexual identity.

We will act with kindness, patience, and respect toward all community members and will strive to create an environment where everyone can contribute.

## Expected Behavior
Contributors and community members are expected to:
- Be respectful and constructive in discussions.
- Assume good intent and respond with patience when disagreements occur.
- Provide clear and actionable feedback.
- Respect the time and effort of others.
- Attribute ideas and credit appropriately.

## Unacceptable Behavior
Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory language toward any person or group.
- Threats of violence or intimidation.
- Sexual content or targeted comments that are not relevant to the project.
- Stalking or encouraging harassment of others.
- Disruption of discussions through trolling, spam, or repeated off-topic posts.
- Any other conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement
Community moderators and maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct.
Enforcement may include:
- Warning the offender or removing abusive content.
- Temporary or permanent bans from the repository and community spaces.
- Reporting serious violations to appropriate parties when necessary.

We may take action at our discretion to protect the community.

## Reporting Guidelines
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, report it by:
- Opening a private security or conduct report (if available), or
- Contacting the project maintainers via the channels listed in `SUPPORT.md`.

Please include:
- The context (what happened, where, and when)
- Links to relevant issues/PRs/comments
- Any additional details that may help moderators assess the situation

## Response Expectations
Reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly. While we may not be able to share details, we will:
- Confirm receipt of the report (when possible)
- Take appropriate action based on the severity of the issue
- Keep the reporter informed about outcome to the extent we can

Thank you for helping us keep EquiPilot AI welcoming and productive.
